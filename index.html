About Me

Who I am and what I do.

Im a Masters student at Iowa State University specializing in DEM-based fertilizer spreader optimization and granular flow modeling. I enjoy connecting simulation, experiments, and machinery design to solve real-world problems in sustainable agriculture.

My work involves EDEM simulations, experimental validation (angle of repose, hopper discharge, impact plate tests), and CAD modeling. Im passionate about building tools and models that help farmers achieve uniform application and efficient field operations.
